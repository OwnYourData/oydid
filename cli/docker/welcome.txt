
Welcome to OYDID (Own Your Decentralized IDentifier)

Installed components:
* oydid - version: ${oydid}
* jq - version: ${jq}

More information: https://github.com/OwnYourData/oydid/

Example:
$ oydid read did:oyd:zQmaBZTghndXTgxNwfbdpVLWdFf6faYE4oeuN2zzXdQt1kh

For a listing of options, use /usr/local/bin/oydid --help.
